Privacy Policy for BuxBack

BuxBack is a Chrome extension designed to provide users with information about potential Robux cashback when purchasing items on Roblox.

Information Collection and Use

BuxBack does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, passwords, or payment details. The extension processes limited non-personal data necessary to calculate and display cashback information.

Data Storage

The extension uses local browser storage to save user preferences and cashback related data to ensure proper functionality and a consistent user experience.

Server Communication

BuxBack communicates with its own servers to retrieve cashback information and handle payout-related functionality. No user data is sold or shared with third parties.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect data and ensure secure communication between the extension and backend services.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at support@buxback.io.
